About me

Sol Cardozo is a dancer, choreographer, and acrobat. She was born in Mar del Plata, Argentina, where she began her artistic training. At the age of 19, she moved to Buenos Aires to study a Bachelor's Degree in Choreographic Composition with a focus on Musical Theater.

She wrote and directed the play “Lápices, un musical con memoria”, which premiered in 2016. The show has received several awards and recognitions and continues to be performed in the Province of La Pampa.

In 2022, she moved to Playa del Carmen, Mexico, where she began her training in aerial arts. She has worked as a dancer for companies such as Barceló and Grand Palladium, among others.
